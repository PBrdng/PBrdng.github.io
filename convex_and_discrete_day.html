Convex and Discrete Day @ FU Berlin. March 11, 2022.


This is the website for the Convex and Discrete Day.
It will take place on March 11, 2022, at FU Berlin.

Description

The Convex and Discrete Day will bring together early-career mathematicians in convex and discrete geometry from Berlin, Leipzig, and Magdeburg. The purpose is to reinvigorate research connections among graduate students and postdocs in the region. The event will take place at FU Berlin and is planned to be in-person.

Registration

If you want to participate, send an email to paul.breiding@mis.mpg.de and frick@cmu.edu.
Due to high hygiene measures we can only accept a restricted number of participants.
